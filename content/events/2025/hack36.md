## About Hack36

**Hack36** is our flagship 36-hour hackathon where innovation meets execution. Teams from across the country come together to build groundbreaking projects with mentorship from industry experts and compete for exciting prizes.

### What Makes Hack36 Special

- **36 Hours** of non-stop coding and innovation
- **Industry Mentorship** from leading tech companies
- **Exciting Prizes** worth lakhs
- **Networking** with fellow developers and recruiters
- **Workshops** and learning sessions

### Tracks

This year we had 5 exciting tracks:
1. **HealthTech** - Solutions for healthcare challenges
2. **EdTech** - Revolutionizing education
3. **FinTech** - Financial technology innovations
4. **Sustainability** - Green tech for a better future
5. **Open Innovation** - Build anything amazing

### Stats from 2025 Edition

- **500+** Participants
- **100+** Teams
- **48** Hours of coding
- **20+** Mentors from industry
