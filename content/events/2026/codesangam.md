## About Codesangam

**Codesangam** is a 2-week development challenge that brings together creativity and code. Build real-world projects, collaborate with peers, and showcase your skills to the community.

### Challenge Format

- **Duration:** 2 weeks of intensive development
- **Teams:** Solo or team participation (max 4 members)
- **Themes:** Different themes each edition
- **Evaluation:** Based on innovation, execution, and impact

### Timeline

1. **Week 1:** Project ideation and development
2. **Week 2:** Refinement and submission
3. **Final Day:** Presentations and judging

### Why Participate

- Build portfolio-worthy projects
- Learn by doing with real-world challenges
- Get feedback from experienced developers
- Win exciting prizes worth Rs. 75,000
- Network with developers from across India

### Registration

Registration opens on **January 15, 2026**. Visit [codesangam.com](https://codesangam.com) for registration and updates.
