## About CC Classes

**CC Classes** are student-led mentorship sessions designed to help freshers and sophomores master the art of programming. From the basics of C++ to advanced Competitive Programming and Development, we cover it all.

### What We Cover
- **Introduction to Programming**: C, C++, and Python basics.
- **Data Structures & Algorithms**: Arrays, Linked Lists, Trees, Graphs, and DP.
- **Web Development**: HTML, CSS, JS, React, and Node.js.
- **App Development**: Flutter and Kotlin.
- **System Design**: Basics of scalable architecture.

### Schedule
Classes are typically held **every week** in the CC Club Room or Lecture Halls. Follow our socials for specific timings!
