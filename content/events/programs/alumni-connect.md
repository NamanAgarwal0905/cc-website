## About Alumni Connect

The **Alumni Industry Connect** series brings back our most successful alumni to share their industry experiences, career advice, and technical insights.

### Why Attend?
- **Real-world Insights**: Learn what it takes to crack interviews at FAANG and startups.
- **Networking**: Connect with seniors working in your dream companies.
- **Technical Deep Dives**: Sessions on System Design, Cloud Computing, and AI/ML trends.

### Past Speakers
We have hosted alumni from **Google, Microsoft, Amazon, Uber, PhonePe**, and high-growth startups.
